Stash — Logo Concepts

Three directions. Each shown at launcher (512), adaptive (108), and notification (48) sizes, on both dark and light backgrounds.

How to evaluate

  • Scale test: look at the 48px column. If it's still readable and distinctive at that size, it'll work as an Android notification icon and favicon.
  • Light background test: your app is dark-themed but the Play Store and user's home screen might not be. A good logo works on both.
  • Symbol-only test: the logo must make sense without the "Stash" wordmark next to it. Check the standalone mark.
  • Retro feeling: which one feels the most "vintage premium" vs "generic modern"?
